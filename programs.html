Academy Programs

We provide structured learning roadmaps for every stage of your development. Accelerate your ELO progression.

Foundations (Beginner)

Master board coordinates, piece movement mechanics, key mating patterns, and basic opening layouts. Perfect for players starting their journey.

Duration: 12 Weeks Learn More

Tactical Mastery (Intermediate)

Develop tactical motifs (forks, pins, skewers), learn king safety protocols, basic endgame geometry, and active positional strategies.

Duration: 16 Weeks Learn More
